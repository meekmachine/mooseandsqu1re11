Welcome to Virtual Health Counseling!



We have gathered a team of skilled virtual health counselors to meet with you to discuss your drinking habits. Get started to:
  • - find out what virtual health counselors are all about
  • - take a confidential screening and get objective feedback about whether the program might be useful for you

After getting feedback from just a few questions, you can decide whether or not to register with us. Once you've registered, you can get:
  • - a comprehensive evaluation
  • - detailed, objective feedback
  • - help with making a decision about whether or not to change your drinking. Our virtual health counselors will not, however, pressure you to change in any way.

If you would like to speak with a virtual counselor now, please click the "Get Started" button. Returning users can access their account by selecting the "Returning User" button.
